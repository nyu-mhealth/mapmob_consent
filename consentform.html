ABOUT THE STUDY

The “MapMob Neighborhood Project" is designed to provide neighborhood residents with a new tool to learn about their day-to-day activities, and to study the way different neighborhood conditions affect the health and well-being of their residents. NYU mHealth is now inviting residents to participate in this community engagement and research project by visiting www.mapmob.com, where they can learn more about the details and consider joining the project.

WHAT IS INFORMED CONSENT?

The purpose of this document is to help you decide whether you would like to join this project. Please scroll through and read this section carefully. To join a research study, you must give what is called your “informed consent.” Informed consent includes [1] reading the information in this section and [2] asking questions about anything that is not clear to you. You should not join this study until all of your questions are answered.

STUDY DESCRIPTION

  • If you agree to be in this study, you will be asked to do the following:
    • Create a basic account at MapMob.com that will allow you to save and monitor your MapMob mobility profile./li>
    • Track your location for using the MapMob smartphone app that you will download on your iPhone or Android phone.
    • You may be offered opportunities to participate in other research studies run by NYU mHealth.
  • You will be asked to provide your phone number and email address so that study researchers can contact you to resolve any technical issues with the app and to remind you to keep the app turned on. Your name and contact information will never be linked to the location information collected by your app.
  • We cannot guarantee that the MapMob app will work on all iPhone or Android models.

RISKS AND BENEFITS

  • The main risks if you participate in the study are the risks of loss of privacy and confidentiality.
  • The risk of your information being seen is extremely low because of strong computer-based security systems.
  • Participants may benefit from increased awareness of the way marketing affects their decision-making about their health.

INCENTIVES

You will not receive any reimbursement for using the MapMob site and app. However, you will be offered opportunities to participate in other research projects which have different amount of reimbursement.

CONFIDENTIALITY

Confidentiality of your research records will be strictly maintained by numerically coding all data and disguising identifying information.

  • By participating in this study you acknowledged that you are older than 18 years.
  • When you withdraw from the study, no information identifying you will be gathered after that date. Information that has already been gathered may still be used.
